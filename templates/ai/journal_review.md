# Journal Review Template

Use this to review saved setup outcomes and extract lessons.

## Inputs

- Saved setup rows
- Outcome stats by pattern
- Outcome stats by R/R quality
- Outcome stats by checklist status
- Outcome stats by readiness bucket

## Rules

- Find repeating mistakes.
- Find conditions that deserve more attention.
- Do not overfit tiny sample sizes.
- Mark low-sample findings as tentative.
